Welcome to Cassandra

There were 3 goals of this project:

One was to automate the creation of a small scale highly available, fault-tolerant, big data infrastructure.
Two was to leverage puppet to perform an stress test and automate the generation of results. Three was to leverage yaml to abstract & parametrize the configurations.

Creating pages manually
